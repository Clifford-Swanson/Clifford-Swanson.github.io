Clifford Swanson, PhD ![CLifford Swanson Faculty Picture](/images/clifford_swanson.jpg)
======
Clifford Swanson is an Assistant Professor at the University of Idaho. They started in the Fall of 2024 in the department of Civil and Environmental Engineering. Prior to this, they received their PhD in civil engineering from the University of Tennessee in 2022, serving as a Post-Doc Researcher and Lecturer there until joining UIdaho. 
Clifford is extremely motivated to mentor the next generation of engineers and researchers, while also managing a highly competitve research program with skills in molecular biology, analytical chemistry, and advanced data science.
