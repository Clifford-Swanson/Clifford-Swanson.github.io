In-refrigerator water dispensing systems are ubiquitous in residential homes withtap wateras the inflow. Passage through these systems resulted in significantmicrobial growthin the water, with the abundance of potential opportunisticpathogensMycobacteriumandPseudomonasincreasing by 8,053- and 221-fold, respectively. Elevated exposure to microbial contaminants linked to in-refrigerator water dispensing systems may represent a significantpublic healthconcern.
